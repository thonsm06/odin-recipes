Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

A hearty bowl of shrimp fried rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 8 Shrimps
  • 1 cup of day old rice
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • green onion for garnish

Steps

  1. Scrambled the egg in a pre-heated wok.
  2. Add the shrimps when the egg lost most of its liquid. Cooked until almost through.
  3. Add rice and all seasonings, heavily stir the wok to aerate all ingredients together.
  4. Turned off the heat and finished with green onion.
