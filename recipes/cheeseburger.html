Cheeseburger

Description

Thick, juicy beef patty in between cheese and soft buns.

Ingredients

  • Wheat buns
  • 1 Beef Patty
  • 1 slice of american cheese
  • condiments of choice
  • *optional a leaf of Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cooked the beef patty on the grill or skillets with medium-high heat, until it has a nice crispy edge.
  2. Stack the beef on the bun with cheese and the optional lettuce.
  3. Add condiments to your liking.
  4. Enjoy your beef.
