Spaghetti

Description

An Italian classic at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese to finish
  • Italian seasonings
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot of water on high until rolling boil.
  2. Cook ground beef on medium heat until well browned, drain some of the fat and add the jar of sauce. Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Boil the pasta once the water is boiling. Drain the pasta when they are al dente as instructed on the package.
  4. Either combine the sauce and pasta in the large pot or serve separately.
  5. Plate with parmesan of choice.
